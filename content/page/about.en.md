Labas (hello in Lithuanian)! My name is Ąžuolas. Here I share thoughts and writings on languages, software engineering, and everyday philosophy, alongside DIY technical guides with detailed examples.

At the end of each post, I included a literary excerpt that I fancy.

---

_Bralei seseris imkiet mani ir skaitikiet\
Ir tatai skaitydami permanikiet_

Translated to English would sound approximately like this:

_Brothers and sisters, take me and read me_\
_And as you read, understand it_

This was written in the archaic Lithuanian language and script by Martynas Mažvydas in the first printed Lithuanian book, the "Catechism" (1547).
